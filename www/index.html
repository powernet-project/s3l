Stanford Sustainable Systems Lab
The Stanford Sustainable Systems Lab is directed by Ram Rajagopal. The Lab's research is on signal processing, statistics, communication and control of sustainable large complex networks. 

S3L is located in the new Y2E2 building of Stanford University.  The building boasts  state of the art architecture to reduce energy and water consumption.
